Our Story

Born from Tradition.
Made for Today.

Sugantra draws its name from the Sanskrit roots su (beautiful, good) and gandha (scent, fragrance) — a nod to the ancient Indian art of gandha shastra, the science of sacred aromas.

Every candle we craft begins with a ritual intention. We source beeswax, coconut wax, and soy wax from sustainable farms, and infuse them with pure essential oils and hand-selected botanicals that have been used in Indian wellness traditions for centuries.

The Sugantra Ritual

More than a Candle.
It's a Practice.

01

Choose Your Intention

Every candle holds an energy. Browse our moods — calm, focus, romance, or energy — and pick the one that speaks to where you are today.

02

Prepare Your Space

Find a quiet spot. Set it away from drafts. Take three slow breaths before lighting, and set your intention for the next few hours.

03

Light & Let Go

Light your Sugantra candle and allow the fragrance to unfold slowly. Don't rush the moment — the burn is the ritual itself.

04

Trim, Care, Repeat

Trim the wick to 5mm before each use. Let the wax pool melt fully during the first burn. A candle well cared for lasts up to 50 hours.
